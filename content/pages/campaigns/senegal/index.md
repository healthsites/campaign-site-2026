## Senegal Health Facility Mapping

![Senegal Medical Regions Map](/theme/images/campaigns/senegal/maps/map-of-senegal-districts.png)
We are working across Senegal's 14 medical regions to validate and update
health facility data in OpenStreetMap.

### Progress

- **Saint-Louis:** Completed (1,265 facilities validated)
- **Tambacounda:** Active campaign (seeking €60,000 funding)
- **Matam:** Planned for 2026
