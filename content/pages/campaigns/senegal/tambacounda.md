## Tambacounda Medical Region Validation Campaign

The Tambacounda region is one of Senegal's most underserved areas for 
healthcare infrastructure. This campaign aims to validate all health 
facilities across the region.

### Campaign Goals

- Validate 450+ health facilities
- GPS verification of all facility locations
- Document services and operational hours
- Train local mappers
- Integrate with national health information system

### Funding Needs

**Total Budget:** €60,000

- Field validation teams: €35,000
- Training and workshops: €10,000
- Equipment (GPS, tablets): €8,000
- Data quality assurance: €7,000
