## Health Facility Mapping in South Sudan

South Sudan has one of the world's most fragile health systems, with acute shortages of facilities, staff, and supplies across all ten states. Reliable health facility data is essential for coordinating the humanitarian response and planning equitable service delivery.

Our campaign begins in Juba, the capital, where population growth and displacement have rendered existing facility maps severely out of date.
