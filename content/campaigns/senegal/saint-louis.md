## Saint-Louis Validation Campaign - Completed

Our pilot campaign in Saint-Louis successfully validated 1,265 health 
facilities across 3 districts.

### Results

- **1,265 facilities** validated
- **3 districts** completed
- **100% GPS verified** locations
- **Integrated** with national MFL

### Impact

This data now supports:
- Emergency response planning
- Healthcare accessibility analysis
- Ministry of Health service planning
- Humanitarian organization operations

### Lessons Learned

The Saint-Louis campaign established our validation methodology now 
being replicated in Tambacounda and other regions.
