## Health Facility Mapping in Mozambique

Mozambique has 11 provinces, each with significant gaps in health facility data. We are working to validate and update records across the country to support emergency response planning and equitable healthcare delivery.

Our initial campaigns focus on the Maputo metropolitan area and the northern province of Nampula, home to Mozambique's two largest urban populations.
