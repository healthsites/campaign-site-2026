## Nampula Province

Nampula is Mozambique's most populous province and a critical corridor for humanitarian logistics in the north. Health facility data across the province is significantly out of date, with many facilities unrecorded or missing key service information.

### Campaign Objectives

- Conduct a full census of health facilities across Nampula's 23 districts
- Map facilities in remote rural areas with no current data coverage
- Integrate findings with national health information systems

### Partners

This campaign will be delivered in partnership with local health authorities and international NGOs operating in northern Mozambique.
