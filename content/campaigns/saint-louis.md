## Saint-Louis — Étape 1 Complete

We ran the Emergency Health Mapping Campaign in Saint-Louis with the local OpenStreetMap community and Senegal's regional health office. 

### Étape 1: Complete

Around 400 health facilities were validated on the ground, and **35 emergency maternal referral facilities** were identified — the places a woman facing complications in childbirth needs to reach.

Every facility was confirmed by trusted field validators, tagged with its source, and published to OpenStreetMap under the Open Database Licence. It is now a permanent public asset that any programme can build on.

### Étape 2: Evidence and Policy Engagement

**Étape 2** focuses on clinical confirmation of the 35 referral facilities, interoperability between the National Statistics Office and the Ministry of Health, and building the cross-ministry business case for open data as national infrastructure. Funding the data collaborative is what keeps this work going and extends it to the next region.
